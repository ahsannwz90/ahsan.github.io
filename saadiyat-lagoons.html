Saadiyat Lagoons by Aldar

Saadiyat Lagoons is the newest residential development by Aldar Properties, featuring luxurious 4, 5, and 6 bedroom villas on the stunning Saadiyat Island in Abu Dhabi. Enjoy the ultimate island lifestyle with eco-friendly mangrove villas that blend seamlessly into the surrounding protected wilderness. Experience an exclusive and serene living environment that offers unlimited access to a plethora of exciting adventures.

Located at the heart of Saadiyat Island, residents can indulge in the rich cultural experiences found in the bustling cultural spine, including delectable cuisine, captivating art, and soul-stirring music. Alternatively, residents can explore the tranquil eco-corniche teeming with wildlife, offering the perfect balance between culture and nature, right outside your doorstep. Discover the beauty of Saadiyat Lagoons and experience luxurious island living at its finest.
